Digital Illustration of me working on my goals

I have always been the kind of person who is a jack of all trades and master of none. Learning to do many things but not ever going one direction and its both a blessing and a curse. I admire people who have the discipline to become incredible at one or even a few things but I also am glad I have the willingness to try so many things even if I end up being mediocre at many of them.

I like taking this class because it is mix of technical and artistic and Photography along with a few other of my hobbies can be like that as well. There is mechanics to what you are doing that are the building blocks you have to learn to manipulate dependent on the situation in order to produce your vision. It can be a frusterating process at times but also quite a rewarding one.
